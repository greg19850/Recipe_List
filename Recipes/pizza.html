Pizza

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.

ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • 0.5 heaping cup pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh bocconcini mozzarella, sliced
  • 10 basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for drizzling

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500Â°F.
  2. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough.
  3. Top with the fresh mozzarella
  4. bake 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is browned.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.
